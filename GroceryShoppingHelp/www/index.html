Grocery Shopping Helper


This app designed by Randal Johnson to assist in the sometimes difficult task of shopping for groceries, when one product gives price per pound, and the competitor's product has the price listed per ounce; also for comparing packs of bottled water, where the number of bottles and bottle size are different.

For questions, comments, or suggestions, please email groceryshoppinghelper@gmail.com
